Inside the Intelligence Engine

Study.AI isn't just a reader—it's an interactive academic copilot. Here is exactly how it transforms your study materials into an active learning experience.

01

Smart Indexing

Smart Indexing

It all starts when you upload a document (PDF, DOCX). Unlike standard PDF readers, Study.AI doesn't just display text; it actively reads and maps it.

  • Vector Database: The AI breaks your textbook down into semantic chunks and stores them in a highly searchable format.
  • Context Awareness: It understands the relationship between chapters, headings, and core concepts.
02
02

The Copilot Interface

The Copilot Interface

Once indexed, the document appears in your dashboard. You can select it and utilize the primary interaction modes via the bottom input bar.

Explain Mode: Highlight a complex paragraph or type a concept from the book. The AI will cross-reference the text and explain it to you simply, acting like a private tutor.

Notes Mode: Command the AI to summarize chapter 4, or extract all key formulas. It instantly outputs highly structured, markdown-formatted study notes.

Active Recall & Testing

Active Recall & Testing

Testing your memory is the best way to learn. Instead of manually writing flashcards, click the Generate Quiz button.

The AI will scan your indexed documents and instantly generate multiple-choice questions, fill-in-the-blanks, and short-answer prompts based entirely on the curriculum you uploaded.
